Vishal's Story

From Mechanical Engineering to regulated pharmaceutical distribution — building in one of the hardest industries in entrepreneurship.

2025 — Present
NovaPharm Healthcare — Founder & CEO

Establishing UK pharmaceutical distribution operations under MHRA and EU-GMP compliance. Building supplier networks across Europe. Navigating regulatory complexity in parallel import and generic medicine distribution. MHRA licensing in process. Operating across UK, Germany, Poland, Romania, Colombia, UAE.

2024
Decision Point — Entering Regulated Industries

Made the uncommon decision to enter regulated pharmaceutical distribution. No background in pharma. No healthcare network. No funding. Just deep research, cold outreach, and operator discipline. Began studying MHRA/EU-GMP frameworks, parallel import regulations, and cross-border pharmaceutical supply chains.

2019-2020
Swiggy — Business Development

Worked at Swiggy during early growth phase. Learned how high-growth startups scale operations. Saw hypergrowth from the inside. Realized the path wasn't mine. Left to build in regulated industries.

2015-2019
ITM University Vadodara — Mechanical Engineering

Studied Mechanical Engineering. Learned systems thinking and analytical frameworks. Realized interest in building businesses rather than machines. Developed technical discipline that would later apply to regulated industries.

Earlier
Foundations — India

Born and raised in India. Educated in Vadodara and Porbandar. Early education at St. Mary's School, Don Bosco High School, and Zenith High School. The foundation that made everything else possible.
