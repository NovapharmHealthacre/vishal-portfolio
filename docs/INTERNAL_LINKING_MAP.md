## Anchor standards

Use descriptive anchors such as “verified public fact sheet”, “current NovaPharm status” and “MHRA parallel-import guidance”. Avoid repetitive exact-match keyword anchors and generic “click here”.

## Orphan controls

All canonical pages are available through the primary navigation, footer, homepage sections or contextual links. Every essay appears in the Thinking collection and has related/pagination navigation.

## Commercial restraint

Personal essays should not end with aggressive corporate sales calls. Relevant readers may move naturally to Ventures, Speaking/Partnerships or Contact when the context supports it.

## Audit procedure

The automated link checker validates local targets and fragments. A quarterly editorial review should confirm that the links remain contextually useful, source URLs remain authoritative and no new page is orphaned.
