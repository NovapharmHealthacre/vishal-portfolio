# Off-site authority plan

Reviewed: 14 July 2026

## Objective

Build legitimate external corroboration around Vishal’s professional identity and ideas. The goal is useful participation in relevant professional conversations, not link acquisition at any cost.

## Priority channels

1. Complete the publisher-hosted Yakuji Nippo series when the fourth article is genuinely live.
2. Contribute source-led guest analysis to established pharmaceutical, regulatory and founder publications.
3. Provide concise expert commentary when a journalist or editor requests insight within the verified public scope.
4. Participate in relevant podcasts, panels or roundtables only when a real invitation and public listing exist.
5. Develop original public frameworks that editors and professionals can reference.
6. Distribute essays through Vishal’s verified LinkedIn profile.
7. Maintain accurate Companies House, corporate website and professional-profile information.

## Outreach qualification

An opportunity is suitable when:

- the publication or event is relevant and has a genuine audience;
- the topic fits Vishal’s verified experience and public-safe knowledge;
- editorial control and attribution are clear;
- no payment is required for a disguised link or fabricated award;
- the content can add original value rather than repeat a generic biography;
- conflicts and commercial relationships can be handled honestly.

## Evidence hierarchy

Strong external authority includes:

- independent coverage about Vishal or his work;
- publisher-edited contributions;
- official event and speaker pages;
- quoted expert commentary;
- credible institutional profiles;
- citations of original frameworks.

Self-published profiles, copied press releases and authored articles should not be misrepresented as independent notability.

## Prohibited tactics

- buying backlinks or awards;
- private blog networks;
- mass guest-post automation;
- fake interviews, podcasts or speaker pages;
- low-quality directory submissions;
- reciprocal-link schemes;
- fabricated media logos;
- automatic outreach from this repository;
- Wikipedia or Wikidata manipulation.

## 90-day action sequence

1. Distribute the existing four essays through the LinkedIn system.
2. Prepare one high-quality guest article pitch derived from a new original framework.
3. Build a private list of no more than ten highly relevant publications or editors.
4. Send personalised outreach only after owner review.
5. Record public outcomes and exact URLs in the fact ledger.
6. Add an external profile to `sameAs` only when identity and control are verified.

## Measurement

Track qualified external mentions, editorial invitations, genuine referring domains, citations, interview requests and professional enquiries. Do not use raw backlink count as the primary success metric.
