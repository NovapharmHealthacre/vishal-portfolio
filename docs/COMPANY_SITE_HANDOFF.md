# Company-site handoff

Reviewed: 14 July 2026

This document prevents the personal founder platform from becoming a substitute for the NovaPharm Healthcare corporate website.

## Boundary

`vishal.novapharmhealthcare.com` is the canonical personal professional platform for Vishal Chakravarty. It may explain his role, founder relationship, writing, public facts and a concise venture status.

`novapharmhealthcare.com` should become the canonical source for the organisation, its approved services, leadership, regulatory status, quality system, partnerships and commercial enquiry journeys.

## Corporate entity contract

The company website should expose and reuse:

- Organisation: `https://novapharmhealthcare.com/#organization`
- Corporate website: `https://novapharmhealthcare.com/#website`
- Founder relationship: `founder` → `https://vishal.novapharmhealthcare.com/#person`
- Legal name: `NOVAPHARM HEALTHCARE LTD`
- Public brand: `NovaPharm Healthcare`
- Company number: `16716501`
- Incorporation date: `2025-09-15`

The corporate website must not create another Person identifier for Vishal. It should reference the personal-domain Person identifier.

## Recommendations for the corporate site only

Do not implement these in the personal portfolio:

1. Corporate About and governance architecture.
2. Verified leadership profiles for approved executives.
3. Service and capability pages that distinguish current, in-progress and planned status.
4. Manufacturer, dossier, CMO/CDMO and distribution partnership journeys.
5. Quality, regulatory and operational-status pages.
6. B2B enquiry forms with privacy, validation, spam protection and source attribution.
7. Corporate editorial policy and Insights hub.
8. Corporate logo, site-name and social-card system using the supplied official logo artwork.
9. Corporate Search Console, Bing Webmaster and measurement configuration.
10. Google Business Profile eligibility review only when a genuinely staffed and qualifying location exists.
11. Corporate sitemap and schema graph.
12. Service-level internal linking and conversion architecture.
13. Corporate IndexNow decision based on actual publishing cadence.
14. Separate regulatory disclaimers and approved capability language.

## Prohibited shortcuts

The company site must not:

- claim an MHRA authorisation before an official record exists;
- represent a registered office as a customer-facing pharmacy, warehouse or operational site;
- publish customer, supplier, NHS, revenue or deployed-technology claims without evidence and permission;
- use LocalBusiness, Pharmacy, MedicalBusiness or WholesaleStore merely for search visibility;
- duplicate the personal essays as corporate articles without an editorial and canonical decision;
- create competing Person or Organization identifiers;
- use the personal portfolio as a corporate lead-generation funnel.
