# Search quality review

Reviewed: 15 July 2026

## Outcome

The portfolio has a strong technical foundation for conventional search, generative search and answer engines. The release maximises legitimate on-site signals without claiming that code can guarantee rankings, AI citations, traffic or a Knowledge Panel.

## Technical search foundation

- Semantic static HTML contains every essential founder, venture, essay and gallery fact before client-side JavaScript.
- One canonical HTTPS origin, consistent trailing slashes, self-canonicals and non-indexable compatibility routes prevent route competition.
- Unique titles, descriptions, H1s, Open Graph metadata, feeds and material sitemap dates are release-tested.
- The canonical Person, ProfilePage, personal WebSite and corporate Organization identifiers remain stable and non-competing.
- Ten substantial public essays provide answer-led, source-backed coverage of pharmaceutical market access, manufacturing, supply and founder execution.
- Googlebot, Bingbot and documented search-retrieval crawlers can reach the public site while model-training preferences remain separate.
- No tracking, doorway content, hidden FAQ copy, fake reviews, fabricated media or unsupported external profiles are used.

## Image-search improvements

- The 1440 × 1402 official portrait is the canonical Person image and a visible gallery image.
- Homepage, About, Founder Profile and Gallery metadata use the high-resolution portrait with truthful dimensions.
- ProfilePage, WebPage and ImageGallery nodes use `primaryImageOfPage` where supported by visible content.
- The gallery exposes eleven crawlable image URLs through normal `<img>` elements and the image sitemap.
- ImageObjects describe Vishal as the subject without inventing photographer or copyright ownership.
- The ten supplied gallery files retain stable descriptive URLs and are not artificially enlarged.

## GEO and AEO foundation

- Visible prose answers the core identity questions: who Vishal is, what he is building, what NovaPharm is and what subjects he writes about.
- Machine-readable facts and structured data mirror visible content rather than forming a separate promotional layer.
- Stable authorship, dates, primary sources, direct summaries and internal links make individual essays independently understandable and citable.
- `facts.json` and `llms.txt` remain supplemental resources; canonical HTML, entity data and sourced essays remain authoritative.

## External limits

The following outcomes cannot be solved or promised by repository changes:

1. Search-engine recrawl, canonical selection and snippet replacement require processing time and should be monitored in Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.
2. AI answers are generated dynamically and may cite different sources, or no source, for different users and queries.
3. A Knowledge Panel depends on Google’s automatically assembled graph and credible corroboration across the wider web.
4. Strong non-branded visibility depends on sustained original publishing and genuine independent references.
5. Higher-resolution originals for the ten supplied gallery portraits would improve image-search and editorial reuse quality.

## Release gates

Every material code change must preserve the deterministic build, canonical/entity tests, claim/privacy guards, image sitemap tests, Chromium/Firefox/WebKit QA, axe accessibility results, three-pass mobile Lighthouse median, desktop Lighthouse thresholds and custom-domain post-deployment verification.
