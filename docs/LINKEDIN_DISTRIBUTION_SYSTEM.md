# LinkedIn distribution system

Reviewed: 14 July 2026

Nothing in this document should be posted automatically. Each post requires owner review and a final check that the linked page is live and accurate.

## Publishing sequence

1. Lead with an original observation, not “I published a new article”.
2. Explain the practical consequence in three to six short paragraphs.
3. Include one concrete framework, distinction or question.
4. Link to the canonical essay once.
5. Invite a professional discussion without engagement bait.
6. Respond to substantive comments with evidence and restraint.
7. Review performance after seven and 28 days using LinkedIn’s own analytics.

## Essay 1: Building in Regulated Industries

**Post draft**

Regulation is often described as the obstacle that appears after the product is built.

In healthcare, that sequence is backwards.

The legal entity, permission, responsible person, site, supplier, record and operating process have to make sense together. A polished interface cannot repair a process that was never authorised or controlled.

The useful form of speed is not making every clock move faster. It is reaching a well-evidenced decision early enough to avoid building the wrong thing.

I wrote about regulation as an operating system, not a final gate:

`https://vishal.novapharmhealthcare.com/essays/regulated-industries/`

Where does your organisation still treat compliance as something added at the end?

**Insight excerpt:** Company registration, permission and operation are different kinds of fact.

**Visual concept:** Four concentric layers labelled Entity, Permission, Process and Evidence.

## Essay 2: What Parallel Import Actually Means

**Post draft**

The price difference is what makes parallel import visible.

It is not what makes the opportunity real.

In the UK, parallel import is a product-specific licensing route. The source product, UK reference product, evidence, packaging, authorised companies and lifecycle obligations all matter. It is not a general permission to move medicines across a border.

The economics start the question. Licensing, quality and supply continuity decide the answer.

A plain-English explanation with current MHRA sources:

`https://vishal.novapharmhealthcare.com/essays/what-parallel-import-actually-means/`

Which part of parallel import is most often mistaken for a shortcut?

**Insight excerpt:** A licence is attached to the product opportunity, not to an entire category.

**Visual concept:** Source product → UK reference → evidence → licence → authorised supply chain.

## Essay 3: How to Win When the Odds Are Against You

**Post draft**

The odds are not always personal. Often, they are structural.

That distinction changes the response.

A weak network can be rebuilt through useful work. Limited capital can force a narrower first move. A lack of public credibility can be answered with evidence rather than louder self-description.

The mistake is copying a better-resourced competitor’s game with fewer resources. The better move is choosing an asymmetric game where the learning survives even if the first experiment fails.

My operating note:

What constraint has forced you to make a better strategic choice?

**Insight excerpt:** Acceptance is not surrender. It is the moment calculation becomes possible.

**Visual concept:** Constraint → asymmetric move → evidence → compounding position.

## Essay 4: The Story Was Too Simple

**Post draft**

Founder stories often become cleaner every time they are retold.

Several years become one decision. Uncertainty disappears. Every earlier experience starts pointing neatly towards the company that exists today.

An earlier version of my own story did that. The surviving evidence did not support the claim that I entered pharmaceuticals with no previous background, so the public record had to change.

Credibility is not the absence of revision. It is the willingness to revise before a contradiction becomes a defence.

The correction remains public here:

`https://vishal.novapharmhealthcare.com/essays/why-i-left-swiggy/`

What makes a founder correction more credible rather than less?

**Insight excerpt:** Contrast is useful in an essay. It is not evidence.

## Cadence

Publish no more than one essay distribution post per week. Alternate with original short observations so the profile does not become a link feed.

## Tracking

Record impressions, meaningful comments, profile views, qualified connection requests, link clicks where LinkedIn provides them and relevant enquiries. Avoid optimising for reactions alone.
