The roadmap defines editorial territories and evidence requirements. It does not authorise publication or claim that planned business capabilities exist.

## Supporting formats

Between major essays, consider:

- one-page frameworks;
- annotated checklists;
- short source notes when official guidance materially changes;
- correction or update notices;
- visual summaries of existing essays;
- publisher-hosted guest contributions where editorially appropriate.

## Portfolio balance

Across the year, maintain:

- at least four regulated-market operating essays;
- at least three market-access or supply essays;
- at least two founder-system essays;
- at least one public correction/evidence review;
- no more than one technology essay unless genuine original insight exists.

## Stop criteria

Do not publish a planned article when it lacks a distinct thesis, primary sources, public-safe examples, author approval or a reason to exist beyond keyword coverage.
