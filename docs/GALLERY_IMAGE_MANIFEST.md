## Quality boundary

The ten supplied gallery files are the only authentic source files available in the repository and local project evidence. They are intentionally displayed near their intrinsic size and are not enlarged or synthetically reconstructed. Higher-resolution originals would materially improve image-search and editorial reuse quality; they can replace the current binaries later without changing public URLs, captions, entity identifiers or page structure.

Publication permission does not prove who photographed or owns copyright in each image. The public structured data therefore identifies Vishal as the subject (`about`) but does not invent `creator`, `copyrightHolder` or `creditText` values.
